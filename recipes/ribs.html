Grilled Ribs

Ribs are a classic American dish that could be eaten year round, but most often cooked in the summer. It's great for a afternoon of grilling, it takes time to cook, but it's worth every delicious bite.

This is a quick recipe, with few ingredients that will leave you glad you tried it. This meal has some prep, but once on the grill you can step away and let it do its thang.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs baby back pork ribs (1-2 racks)
  • Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Let ribs sit at room tem for 30 min. Cut pork between rib bones into two sections. Season both sides with S&P.
  2. Prepare your has or charcoal grill for med heat.
  3. Grill rib bone side down until golden brown, about 15 min. Turn ribs and continue to grill until golden brown and crisp, about 15 min more.
  4. Cut between bones and serve on platter.
