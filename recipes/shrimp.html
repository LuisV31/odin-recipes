Grilled Shrimp with Old Bay and Aioli

You can't go wrong with this grilled shrimp recipe if you want something delicious and fast. With only a few ingredients you can pair this shrimp up with a side dish and have dinner ready in less than an hour.

Seasoned with Old Bay, a little garlic and some lemons, these grilled shrimp give off a classic East Coast vibe. I recommend you keep the shell on, the crunchy texture and flavor add so much to this dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of shell-on shrimp
  • 3 small garlic cloves, finely grated, divided
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning, plus more for serving
  • 3/4 tsp. kosher salt
  • large egg
  • 2 lemons

Steps

  1. Prepare a grill for medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grate. The shells are a protective barrier, so keep them on when grilling.
  2. Transfer shrimp to a medium bowl. Add oil, garlic, Old Bay, salt and toss to combine. let sit while you prepare the aioli.
  3. Whisk egg yolk and remaining garlic in a medium bowl. Grate lemon zest into egg mixture. Whisk constantly, gradually stream in oil until thick. Stir in juice of one lemon half. Season well with salt
  4. Cut lemon in half. Grill shrimp and 3 lemon halves until brown and charred in spots.
  5. Spread aioli on a platter, Arrange shrimp and charred lemons. Season lightly with more Old Bay
